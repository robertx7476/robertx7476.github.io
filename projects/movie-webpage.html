Movie Webpage Project

We all decided that the genres for our webpage will be action and horror. The title of our webpage was turn around and it was about Jaxson in hgihschool being ignored by his friends, so he make irrational decisions and wanted to murder all his friends. And we all worked well together and we helped each other with the code.

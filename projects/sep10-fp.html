Sep 10 Freedom Project

The Freedom Project is a year long project for sophomores, at the beginning of the year we had to choose a topic that we are going to research on. The topic I chose was environment. I chose environment because I care about the environment and I like nature and animals. So I decided to research some technologies and innovations that are helping our environment and some future technologies that could exist or almost exists.

In this project, we were suppose to make something using our tool. The tool I chose was A-Frame. But I didn't create anything with A-Frame because I didn't know what to create.

But after everything I know what I would've done differently. One thing would be using my tool and I should've made a prototype of what a small CCS machine could be in the future. And another thing I would have done differently was I would've made my topic a little more specific. There weren't any major challenges in this project, I knew how to use HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap and A-Frame wasn't that hard to learn.

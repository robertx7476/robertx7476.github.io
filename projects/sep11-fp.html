My Freedom Project

For my Freedom Project in my junior year, my partners and I created a mini-game that is farm themed. We used p5play and Kaboom.js to create our project. We faced many obstacles when we were coding our project. One obstacle was that p5play and Kaboom didn't want to merge with each other. We overcame that with links and instead we seperated the title screen and the main game. I do wish I would've done more research on my tool which we didn't use because it wasn't needed and because it wasn't working. My tool was impact.js and currently in 2025, when I checked the pages of the website. I didn't realize at first but it said that Impact wasn't maintained for 14 years and that it's not going to get any updates. So I wasn't able to use my tool properly but only one of my classmate actually got the tool to work. The rest of my classmates switched their tools or they just didn't use impact.
